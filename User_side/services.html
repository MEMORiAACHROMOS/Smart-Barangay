Below are the healthcare services currently offered by Barangay Baesa Health Center.

General Checkup - Routine medical consultation and health assessment.

Pediatric Consultation - Medical care for infants, children, and adolescents.

Prenatal Care - Monitoring and support for pregnant mothers.
